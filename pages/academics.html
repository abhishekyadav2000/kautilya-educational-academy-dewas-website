[Classroom Teaching Image]

CBSE Curriculum Framework

Kautilya Educational Academy follows the curriculum prescribed by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. Our academic program is designed to provide a strong foundation in core subjects while encouraging exploration and critical thinking.

We adopt the National Curriculum Framework (NCF) guidelines that emphasize:

  • Connecting knowledge to life outside the school
  • Ensuring learning shifts from rote methods
  • Enriching the curriculum beyond textbooks
  • Making examinations more flexible and integrated
  • Nurturing an overriding identity informed by caring concerns

Ages 3-6 years | Foundation for lifelong learning

Our pre-primary program focuses on play-based learning that develops cognitive, social, emotional, and physical skills. Children learn through exploration, creative activities, and structured play.

Key Focus Areas:

  • Language development and early literacy
  • Number sense and basic mathematics
  • Environmental awareness
  • Art, craft, and creative expression
  • Physical development and motor skills
  • Social skills and emotional intelligence

Assessment & Evaluation

Comprehensive evaluation system for holistic development

Continuous & Comprehensive Evaluation

We follow the CBSE's assessment pattern that evaluates students holistically:

Scholastic Assessment:

  • Periodic Tests and Unit Tests
  • Half-Yearly and Annual Examinations
  • Projects and Assignments
  • Practical Work and Laboratory Assessments
  • Notebook Maintenance

Co-Scholastic Assessment:

  • Work Education
  • Art Education
  • Health & Physical Education
  • Discipline and Attendance
