Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are the school timings? ‚ñº
School timings are 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM for classes I to V, and 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM for classes VI to XII. Pre-primary section operates from 9:00 AM to 12:30 PM.
How can I schedule a campus visit? ‚ñº
You can schedule a campus visit by calling our admissions office at +91 9926042015 or by filling out the contact form above. Visits are usually scheduled on weekdays between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM.
What is the procedure to meet the Principal? ‚ñº
Parents can request an appointment with the Principal through the school reception. Prior appointment is necessary except in emergency situations.
How do I report student absence? ‚ñº
Parents should inform the class teacher about student absence via the school diary or by calling the school reception. For medical leave, a medical certificate should be submitted upon return.
Is there a parent portal for online access? ‚ñº
Yes, we have a parent portal where you can check attendance, fee status, exam results, and circulars. Login credentials are provided at the time of admission.
