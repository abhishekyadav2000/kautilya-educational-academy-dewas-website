Admission Overview

Join the Kautilya family for a transformative educational experience.

Kautilya Educational Academy welcomes applications from students who are eager to learn and grow. We believe in providing equal opportunities to all students and selecting candidates who will benefit from and contribute to our school community.

Admissions are open for the academic year 2026-27 for classes Nursery to XII. We encourage early applications as seats are limited and allocated on a first-come, first-served basis after fulfilling eligibility criteria.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about admissions

When does the admission process start? ‚ñº
Admissions for the new academic session typically begin in January and continue until seats are available. We recommend applying early to secure a seat.
Is there an entrance test for admission? ‚ñº
For Nursery to Class I, we conduct an informal interaction with the child and parents. For Classes II onwards, there is a written assessment to evaluate the student's readiness for the class applied.
What is the medium of instruction? ‚ñº
English is the primary medium of instruction at Kautilya Educational Academy. Hindi is taught as a compulsory subject.
Is transport facility available? ‚ñº
Yes, we provide safe and comfortable transport facility covering major areas of Dewas. Transport charges are separate from tuition fees.
Can I visit the school before applying? ‚ñº
Absolutely! We encourage parents to schedule a campus visit. Please contact our admission office to book an appointment for a guided tour of our facilities.
