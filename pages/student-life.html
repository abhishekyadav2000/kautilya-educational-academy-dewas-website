Life at Kautilya

Where learning meets living, and every day brings new opportunities.

At Kautilya Educational Academy, we believe that education extends far beyond the classroom. Our vibrant student life program ensures that every child discovers their passions, develops new skills, and creates lasting memories.

From early morning assemblies to exciting after-school activities, students experience a rich tapestry of learning, creativity, sports, and social interaction. We nurture not just academic excellence but also leadership, teamwork, creativity, and compassion.

Every student is encouraged to participate in co-curricular activities that align with their interests and talents, helping them grow into well-rounded individuals ready to take on the world.

Student Council

Developing tomorrow's leaders today

Our Student Council provides opportunities for students to develop leadership skills and take responsibility.

Elected by their peers, council members serve as role models and help in organizing school events, maintaining discipline, and representing student interests.

Council Positions:

  • Head Boy & Head Girl
  • Deputy Head Boy & Deputy Head Girl
  • House Captains & Vice Captains
  • Class Prefects & Monitors
  • Club Presidents & Secretaries

The investiture ceremony at the beginning of each academic year is a proud moment when new leaders take their oath of responsibility.
